About Mundada Polymer

Welcome to Mundada Polymer, where we bring innovation and excellence to your packaging needs. With the successful journey of Baikakaji Polymers, Bai-Kakaji Group proudly presents our latest venture - Mundada Polymer, your ultimate destination for all things packaging!

We offer a wide range of top-quality packaging solutions, including Surface Protection Film, Guard Film, Masking Film, LDPE Shrink Film, Stretch Hood Film, Stretch Wrap Film, LDPE Tubing Film Roll, Shrink Hood Film, PE Milk Film, VCI – Stretch Wrap Film, and Silage Wrap Film. Our products are designed to meet the highest industry standards, ensuring that your packaging requirements are met with utmost satisfaction.

But that's not all! At Mundada Polymer, we believe in going beyond just providing products. Our mission is to create an environment where our customers are not just customers, but valued partners. Our team of experts is always ready to assist you with your queries and problems, providing comprehensive solutions and exceptional customer service. We are committed to building long-lasting relationships with our customers, based on trust, reliability, and mutual success.

So, join us at Mundada Polymer and experience packaging solutions. Let us be your trusted partner in meeting all your packaging needs with unparalleled quality, service, and commitment.
