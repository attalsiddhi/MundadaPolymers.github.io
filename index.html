STRETCH HOOD FILM

A stretch hood is a tube of film sealed on one end, which is stretched over a palletized load to secure the contents to the pallet. The film is cut to the appropriate length, heat sealed on the top end, This type of packaging method for pallet provides many advantagesranging from cost reductions to handling and marketing potential Stretch hoods films are applicable in a wide range of industries such as; Chemical & Polymer, Building & Construction, Food & Drink and Logistic & Transport. Vertical or horizontal usage.

SPECIFICATIONS :
Width : 1300 - 2500 mm
Thickness : 50 - 150 microns
Colour : Clear / Transparent, Blue, White etc.
APPLICATIONS :
  • Textile / Yarn / Fiber
  • Beverages
  • Chemical
  • Pharmaceutical
  • Papers
  • Building & Construction
  • FMGG
  • Packaging
  • Lubricants industries
  • Polymer industries
  • Glass bottle etc
STRETCH HOODS FILM ARE USED FOR :
  • Transport packaging of palletized products (primary, secondary or tertiary packaging)
  • Packaging of separate products such as white goods or the like (primary packaging )
  • When it is not possible to use stretch
  • The primary advantage is its elasticity with high rebuilding forces.

LDPE SHRINK FILM

LDPE Shrink Film is mainly used for unitizing and bundling multiple units of products for better handling and ease of distribution. Our Shrink Film is also used on automatic shrink-wrapping machines. We offer customized shrink films to cater variety of customer varying on their requirements. The tight wrapping of the pack enables high stacking of packs over each other thus saving space and reduce damage in transit

SPECIFICATIONS :
Width : Upto 2500 mm
Thickness : 50 to 150 Microns
APPLICATIONS :
  • Beverages (cans, glass and plastic bottles)
  • Mineral Bottled water / Packaged drinking water
  • Food and canned goods
  • Pharmaceutical ,Health & beauty aids
  • Automobile & Industrial products
  • Batteries
  • Large & small household appliance
  • Packaging materials & paper etc

SURFACE PROTECTION FILM / GUARD FILM / MASKING FILM

We offer a comprehensive line of surface protection films that are designed to minimize abrasion, scratching, and damage throughout the storage process as well as during the delivery process.

SPECIFICATIONS :
Width : 100 - 1500 mm
Thickness : 35 - 150 microns
Colour : Clear / Transparent, Blue, White, Black & White etc
APPLICATIONS :
  • Stainless steel sheets
  • Colour coated steel sheets
  • Aluminium sheets, profiles and composite panels
  • Glass
  • Decorative laminates
  • Furniture
  • Automotive and automobiles
  • Ceramic Tiles
  • Carpet
  • Appliance Industries etc
ADVANTAGES OF SURFACE PROTECTION :
  • Wrinkle and crease free, hence gives uniform coverage to the masked surface.
  • Keeps surface / sheets bright and shiny
  • Does not damage the masked / protected surface or carry away the coating along with it
  • Economical and easy to use
  • Easy peel off.
  • Environment friendly and 100 % recyclable
  • Provides weather resistance when used outside
  • When the protective layer is removed, the quality of the surface is preserved without leaving any residue or glue / mark.
